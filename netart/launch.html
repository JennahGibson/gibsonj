Net.Art Launch Page

The Net.Art assignment is about showing us aspiring designers how art functions outside of the classroom. By researching and brainstorming ideas for these Net.Art pages, we expand our minds as well as our knowledge on CSS and Html.

This process book demonstrates my own brainstorming and drafting processes for this assignnment. The first step in this assignment was to draft a phrase in which the entire Net.Art project would be based around. It took many days to come up with ideas for the general topic that I drafted up for the assignment. My phrase was..

“Owners becoming pavers becoming vendors with leaving extremism trucks.”
